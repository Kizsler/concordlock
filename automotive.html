Automotive Locksmith

Keys, Fobs & Ignitions for Every Make

Modern cars are rolling computers — and their keys are too. Concord Locksmith programs transponder keys and remote fobs for most makes and models, repairs or replaces ignitions, and responds quickly to emergency lockouts. Save a tow to the dealership and hundreds of dollars in the process.

We come to you when we can — roadside lockout help, parking lot key programming, even ignition repair at your home or office. Our technicians carry dealer-grade programming tools for most vehicles built after 1995.

Full-Service Automotive

Eight automotive services — most completed same-day, at a fraction of dealer cost.

01

Key & Remote Programming

Transponder keys, smart keys, proximity fobs, and remote controls programmed on-site for most vehicles.

02

Emergency Car Lockout

Keys locked inside? We get you in without damaging your door, window, or weatherstripping.

03

Ignition Switch Repair

Key won't turn? Stuck in the ignition? We repair worn or damaged ignition cylinders — often cheaper than a dealer.

04

Ignition Replacement

When repair isn't possible, we replace the ignition and rekey it to match your existing keys.

05

Lost Key Replacement

Lost your only key? We make a new one, program it to your car, and deactivate the missing one for security.

06

Key Duplication

Standard car keys, high-security laser-cut keys, and transponder duplicates — cut and programmed in-shop.

07

Broken Key Extraction

Key snapped off in the door or ignition? We remove the broken piece and restore function without lock replacement.

08

Car Lock Rekeying

Bought a used car? Rekey it to new keys so prior owners can't access your vehicle.
