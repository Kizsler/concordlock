A Locksmith Just North of Walnut Creek

Walnut Creek sits just south of our shop — a quick run down Ygnacio or Treat from 1804 Colfax Street. We've served the city's mix of busy downtown retail, the Broadway Plaza commercial core, and established neighborhoods like Rossmoor, Northgate, and Saranap for years, the same family-owned shop that's been in Concord since 1947.

Whether you're a Walnut Creek homeowner who just moved in, a downtown business that needs a door or lock fixed, or an HOA managing common-area hardware, you get the same crew: licensed, bonded, marked vehicle, and a quote before the work starts.
