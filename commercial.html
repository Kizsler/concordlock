Commercial Locksmith

Security That Keeps Your Business Running

Offices. Storefronts. Warehouses. Multi-tenant buildings. Concord Locksmith has protected Contra Costa County businesses for three generations — with professional-grade hardware, master key systems, and modern access control. We work around your schedule to minimize disruption, and we stand behind every installation.

From a single door closer to a full multi-building master key system, we bring commercial-grade expertise and honest pricing. If you've inherited a mystery key system or need to lock down access after an employee change, we can help today.

Custom-designed key hierarchies for offices, rentals, and multi-building properties. One key for you, specific keys for staff.
