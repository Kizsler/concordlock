Residential Locksmith

Protecting the Homes of Contra Costa County

Your home is your family's safe place. Since 1947, Concord Locksmith has helped homeowners across Contra Costa County keep it that way — with honest advice, expert workmanship, and same-day service for emergencies. Whether you've just moved in, lost a key, or want to upgrade to smart locks, we handle every residential job with the same care we'd give our own homes.

Every technician is licensed, bonded, and trained on the latest residential hardware. We carry a full inventory of locks, deadbolts, and smart devices — so most jobs are done in a single visit.

Full-Service Residential

Eight core services every homeowner should know about — all performed by licensed, bonded technicians.

01

Emergency Lockout

Locked out of your house? We arrive fast and get you back inside without damaging your door or hardware.

02

Lock Rekeying

Moved into a new home? Rekey all your locks to a single new key — cheaper than replacement and just as secure.

03

Lock Repair & Replacement

Sticky keys, wobbly knobs, broken locks — we repair when possible and replace with quality hardware when it isn't.

04

Deadbolt Installation

Add a deadbolt to any door — our most-requested security upgrade. Installed clean, fit right, built to last.

05

Smart Lock Installation

Keypad, Bluetooth, Wi-Fi, or app-controlled locks from brands we trust. We help you choose, install, and set up.

06

Key Duplication

Extra keys cut while you wait — standard keys, high-security keys, and restricted keyway duplication.

07

Security Assessments

Not sure where your home is vulnerable? We'll walk through your property and recommend honest, practical upgrades.

08

Broken Key Extraction

Key snapped off in the lock? We extract the broken piece cleanly and get your lock working again — often without replacement.
