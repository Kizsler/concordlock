Keyless Entry & Access Control

Modern Security, No Keys Required

Keys get lost, copied, and forgotten. Modern access control fixes all three — with keypads, cards, fobs, and smartphone entry that you can manage remotely. Concord Locksmith designs, installs, and maintains keyless entry systems for homes, offices, rental properties, and multi-tenant buildings across Contra Costa County.

Want to grant guest access for a weekend? Disable an ex-employee's credentials in seconds? Get an alert when the back door opens? Keyless systems give you control that physical keys never could. We'll help you pick the right solution for your property — simple keypad or full enterprise system.

What's Included

Complete Access Control

Eight keyless capabilities — from simple keypad locks to fully-managed multi-door systems.

01

Stand-Alone Keypad Entry

Battery-powered keypad locks — no wiring, no hub. Perfect for single-door setups at homes and small offices.

02

Push-Button Access

Mechanical push-button locks — no batteries, no electronics, unlimited code changes. Classic simplicity.

03

Card Credential Systems

Prox cards and key fobs for offices, gyms, apartment buildings — easy to issue, instantly revocable.

04

PIN Credential Systems

Assign unique PIN codes to employees, tenants, or family members — track entry, disable instantly.

05

Remote Access Management

Cloud-based systems let you grant, revoke, and schedule access from anywhere — phone, laptop, tablet.

06

Smart Lock Integration

August, Yale, Schlage, Kwikset — we install and configure smart locks that work with your home system.

07

Multi-Door Systems

Enterprise access control for commercial properties — multiple doors, user groups, schedules, audit logs.

08

System Maintenance

Battery replacement, firmware updates, credential management, and troubleshooting — we service what we install.
