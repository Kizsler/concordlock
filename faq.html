Ask Us Anything

The Questions Concord Actually Asks

These are the questions we hear at the counter and on the phone every week. If yours isn’t here, call (925) 689-1534 or send us a message — a real person at the Colfax Street shop answers both.

01

How much does a locksmith cost in Concord?

It depends on the job — a straightforward rekey is very different from opening a jammed safe. What we can promise is how pricing works: we quote before we work, the quote is the price, and there are no after-the-fact “service fees.” That’s been our policy for 75+ years. Beware of any locksmith advertising a too-good-to-be-true price — that’s the classic bait-and-switch setup in this industry.

02

Do you make car keys? Even the chip kind?

Yes. We cut and program transponder keys, laser-cut keys, and remote fobs for most makes and models — usually for less than the dealership and often the same day. Bring your registration and photo ID; we verify ownership before originating any automotive key.

03

Should I rekey or replace my locks?

If your locks are in good mechanical shape, rekeying is usually the smart choice: we reset the pins so old keys stop working, and you keep your existing hardware. Replacement makes sense when locks are worn, damaged, or you want to upgrade security. We’ll tell you honestly which one your doors need.

04

I just bought a house in Concord. What should I do first?

Rekey everything. You have no idea how many copies of your keys exist — previous owners, contractors, neighbors, agents. One visit makes every old key dead. It’s the single highest-value security step for a new homeowner.

05

Are you a real local shop?

Yes — walk in and see us at 1804 Colfax Street in downtown Concord, across from the Sugar Plum Diner. Family-owned since 1947, three generations. We’re not a call center that dispatches whoever’s nearby, which is how most locksmith scams work.

06

What should I check before hiring any locksmith?

Three things: a real local address you could walk into, a quote given before the work starts, and a technician who arrives in a marked vehicle with credentials. If any of those is missing, hang up — even if it’s not us you call instead.

07

Do you handle after-hours emergencies?

If you’re locked out, call us at (925) 689-1534 — don’t wait for business hours. Shop hours are Mon–Wed & Fri 8:30–5, Thu 9:30–5, but emergency service is available beyond them.

08

What area do you serve?

Concord first — it’s in the name — plus the surrounding Contra Costa County communities. Mobile service comes to you; in-shop work like key cutting and safe service happens at the Colfax Street shop.

09

Do you sell safes I can actually look at?

Yes — our shop doubles as a safe showroom: gun safes, fire safes, drop safes, and home safes you can open and compare in person. We deliver, install, and service what we sell.

10

Can you fix a smart lock someone else installed?

Almost always. Most smart-lock problems are actually door problems — alignment, backset, worn hardware — and fixing doors is our trade. We service keyless hardware we sold and hardware we didn’t.
