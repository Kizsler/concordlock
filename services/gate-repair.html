Gates & Perimeter

Gates That Lock the Way They Should

A gate that won't latch or lock isn't just an annoyance — for an HOA, an apartment complex, or a business yard, it's a security and liability problem. We repair and replace gate locks, latches, and locking mechanisms throughout Concord and Contra Costa County.

From a single side-yard gate to a community's automatic entry gate, we get the hardware working and keep it that way — and we'll flag anything that's a safety or compliance concern while we're there.

01

Gate Locks & Latches

Worn, jammed, or broken gate locks and latches repaired or replaced with hardware rated for outdoor use and daily traffic.

02

Automatic & HOA Gates

Locking mechanisms for automatic and community gates serviced and repaired so residents and vehicles get through — and others don't.

03

Commercial Gate Mechanisms

Yard, lot, and facility gate locking hardware for businesses — built to hold up to weather and heavy use.

04

Hardware Compliance

We'll point out gate hardware that's a safety or code concern, so an HOA or property manager can address it before it becomes a problem.

Who We Help

From One Gate to a Whole Community

Most gate calls come from the people responsible for shared access — HOA boards, apartment and condo managers, and businesses with yards or lots. A gate that fails locks residents out, lets the wrong people in, or becomes a liability the moment someone gets hurt on faulty hardware. We treat it with the same urgency you do.

HOAs & Communities

Pool gates, courtyard gates, and community entrances — serviced so residents get through with their key or fob and the gate actually secures behind them.

Apartments & Condos

High-traffic pedestrian and vehicle gates take a beating. We repair and reinforce the locking hardware that wears out fastest.

Businesses & Yards

Lots, storage yards, and facility gates secured with weather- and traffic-rated hardware that holds up to daily commercial use.
