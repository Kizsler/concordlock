The Details

A Real Safe Showroom in Downtown Concord

Buying a safe online means guessing about size, weight, and fire rating from photos. Our shop at 1804 Colfax Street is a real showroom: you can open the doors, work the locks, and compare gun safes, fire safes, drop safes, and home safes in person before you buy. We deliver and install in Concord and throughout Contra Costa County — safes are heavy, and stairs, doorways, and flooring matter.

Already own a safe? We service what we sell and what we don't. Combination changes after a move or a change in who should have access, electronic keypad repair and replacement, dial mechanism service, and periodic maintenance that keeps a safe opening smoothly for decades.

And when a safe won't open — lost combination, dead keypad, jammed bolt work — we open it with the least invasive method that will work, with the goal of returning the safe to service rather than destroying it. Proof of ownership is required for any safe opening.

Choosing a gun safe?

California sets standards for firearm storage devices. We'll walk you through sizes, fire ratings, and lock types in the showroom so the safe fits both your firearms and your space.

Inherited a locked safe?

It happens more often than you'd think. With proof of ownership, we can open it, service it, and set a fresh combination so it's yours in practice, not just on paper.

Business cash handling?

Drop safes and depository safes let staff secure cash without opening the main compartment. We supply, install, and service them for Concord businesses.
