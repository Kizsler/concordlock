Electronic Entry

Control Who Gets In — and When

Keys are simple, but sometimes you need more: an audit trail of who came and went, the ability to remove someone's access in seconds, or an entry system that doesn't hand out a physical key at all. We install and service access control and intercom systems for businesses, apartments, schools, and HOAs across Concord and Contra Costa County.

Just as important — we repair them. When a fob reader dies or an intercom goes silent, people are locked out and a building's security is compromised. We get electronic entry working again, whether we installed it or not.

01

Key Fob & Card Access

Proximity fobs and card readers for offices, apartment buildings, and shared entries — installed, programmed, and managed.

02

FOB Reader Repair

Fob systems fail, and when they do, residents and staff are stuck outside. We diagnose and repair failed readers and controllers fast.

03

Electronic Door Access

Keypad, fob, and smartphone-based entry for individual doors or whole buildings — with the wiring and door hardware done right.

04

Intercom Systems

Audio entry intercoms for apartment buildings and businesses — installed and serviced so visitors can reach the right person and get buzzed in.

05

School & Classroom Intercom

Audio intercom for schools and classrooms, supporting controlled-entry and communication needs. We install and service systems suited to campus environments.

06

Credential Management

Add or revoke fobs, cards, and codes as people come and go — no rekeying, no handing out new keys.
