The Details

Buy It From Someone Who Can Explain It

Pepper spray, stun guns, and personal alarms are easy to buy online and easy to buy wrong. At our downtown Concord shop you can hold the product, learn how it actually works, and get straight answers about what California law allows before you spend a dollar.

We stock compact pepper sprays that fit on a keychain, stun devices, and personal alarms suited for students, runners, real estate agents, and anyone who works late or walks to their car alone. Staff will show you how to carry, arm, and use each product — the part the online listing never teaches you.

It's a natural fit for a locksmith: our whole trade is helping Concord families feel secure. The same counter that cuts your house key can outfit you, your kids heading to college, or your evening-shift employees with something practical for personal safety.

First time buying pepper spray?

We'll explain the difference between stream and cone sprays, effective range, and how to check the expiration date — yes, pepper spray expires.

Gift for a student?

Keychain sprays and personal alarms are the most popular send-off gifts for college-bound kids. Stop in and we'll put together a sensible kit.

Questions about legality?

California regulates self-defense products — sizes, ages, and where you can carry. We stay current so what you buy at our counter is legal to own and carry here.
