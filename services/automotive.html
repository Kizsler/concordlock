The Details

Car Keys Without the Dealership

Lost your only car key? Locked it inside? Before you call the dealership and wait days for an appointment, call the shop on Colfax Street. We cut and program transponder keys, laser-cut keys, and remote fobs for most makes and models — usually for considerably less than a dealer visit, and often the same day.

Modern car keys are computers: the chip inside has to be programmed to your vehicle before it will start the engine. We have the equipment to do that programming in-house, which is why a locksmith can replace a "dealer-only" key at all. Bring your car to our downtown Concord shop, or have our mobile technician come to you anywhere in Concord and Contra Costa County.

We also repair worn ignitions that grind, stick, or refuse to turn — replacing an ignition cylinder is routine work for us and far cheaper than letting a dealership replace the whole assembly.

Locked out of your car?

Don't pry the door or break a window — modern weather seals and wiring make DIY entry expensive. Call (925) 689-1534 and wait somewhere safe.

Lost every key?

We can originate a new key from the vehicle itself — no existing key needed. Bring your registration and photo ID; we verify ownership before cutting any automotive key.

Spare key while you wait

The cheapest insurance against a lockout is a spare cut before you need it. Stop by the shop — most duplicates are done while you wait.

Business & Fleet

Keys for Work Trucks & Fleets

If your business runs vehicles, you know the math: a single dealer key for a work truck can cost a fortune and a day of downtime. We cut and program keys and fobs for fleet and commercial vehicles — including push-to-start (proximity) systems — so you keep spares on hand and trucks on the road.

Fleet & Work Truck Keys

Extra and replacement keys for vans, trucks, and fleet vehicles — cut and programmed on-site or in our shop, usually for far less than the dealership.

Push-to-Start Programming

Lost or need a spare for a keyless push-to-start vehicle? We program proximity fobs for most makes and models.

Spares Before You Need Them
