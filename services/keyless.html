The Details

Stop Carrying Keys You Don't Need

Keypads, fobs, and smartphone-connected locks have gone from novelty to mainstream — and installed correctly, they're genuinely more convenient and just as secure as a traditional deadbolt. We install and service keyless entry for homes and businesses across Concord and Contra Costa County.

For homes, a keypad deadbolt means the kids never lose a key, the dog walker gets a code you can delete later, and you stop hiding spares under the mat. For businesses, electronic access means you can give every employee their own code or card, see who comes and goes, and remove access in seconds when someone leaves — no rekeying required.

The part most people get wrong is the door, not the lock: alignment, backset, and hardware condition decide whether a smart lock works smoothly or fights you every night. Because we're locksmiths first, we fix the door issues as part of the installation — something an app installer won't do.

Which smart lock should you buy?

Bring your questions — or a photo of your door — to the shop at 1804 Colfax Street. We'll tell you what actually fits your door and your habits before you spend anything.

Keypad acting up?

Dead batteries, worn keypads, and lost programming codes are quick fixes for us. We service keyless hardware we sold and hardware we didn't.

Mixing old and new?

Most homes run a smart lock on the main door and traditional locks elsewhere. We'll key everything sensibly so the whole house still works together.
