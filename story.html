Family-Owned for Over 75 Years.

The unexpected happens, and that's why we're here. Concord Locksmith is family owned and operated since 1947. Our licensed and bonded technicians are courteous, professional and well-trained. We stand behind our work 100%.

Concord Locksmith has been part of this community for over 75 years — still family-owned, still in downtown Concord, built on the same foundation of honesty and honest work that started in 1947.

We're not a call center. We're your neighbors. Every technician carries full credentials, arrives in a marked vehicle, and backs every job with a complete satisfaction guarantee.
