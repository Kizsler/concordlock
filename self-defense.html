Self Defense

Carry Confidence, Not Fear

Security starts with locks on your door — but it doesn't end there. Concord Locksmith carries a curated selection of personal protection products: pepper spray, stun guns, and safety devices from trusted manufacturers. We believe protection should be accessible to everyone, and we back every product we sell with honest guidance on how to use it safely and legally in California.

Stop in and our knowledgeable staff will walk you through the options, explain California law, and help you pick what fits your life. We carry everything from keychain-sized pepper spray to heavy-duty stun batons — quality brands only.
